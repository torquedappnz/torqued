# Torqued — Mechanic Cold Call Script

---

## BEFORE YOU DIAL

**Know this:** You're not selling software — you're offering them more booked jobs and zero admin headaches. Lead with the value, close with the trial offer.

**Preparation:**
- Look up the workshop on Google Maps / Facebook first
- Note: do they have reviews? Are they listed on Autotrader? Are they solo or a team?
- Best call times: Tuesday–Thursday, 7:30–9:00 AM or 4:30–5:30 PM

---

## OPENING (0–20 sec) — Get past the gate

> **"Hey, is [Owner Name / 'the owner'] around? It's [Your Name] from Torqued — we're a Dunedin-based platform that books car jobs for mechanics here. Quick 90-second call for them."**

_If gatekept:_
> **"Totally fine — can I grab a name and call back tomorrow? We've got something useful for the workshop, just want 90 seconds."**

---

## HOOK (20–60 sec) — Make them curious

> **"So basically — we send you booked jobs from customers in your service area. You don't pay per lead, you don't do any chasing. Customer books online, pays a deposit, you get the job details. We take 4% on completion and that's it."**

> **"Most mechanics on the platform are pulling in 2–5 extra jobs a week — oil changes, WOFs, diagnostics — that they weren't getting before."**

---

## PAIN PROBE (60–90 sec) — Let them talk

> **"Quick question — how are you finding your bookings at the moment? Are you sitting at full capacity most weeks, or are there days where the bays could use more work?"**

_Listen. Let them answer. Common responses:_

- "We're pretty busy" → **"Perfect, so you'd be able to cherry-pick the jobs that suit you — we let you decline anything that doesn't fit."**
- "It's hit and miss" → **"That's exactly who we built this for. Slow weeks fill up fast once the platform's running your area."**
- "How is it different from X?" → **"We're local-only — Dunedin customers, Dunedin mechanics. No overseas competition, no price wars."**

---

## OFFER (90–120 sec) — The close

> **"We're onboarding Dunedin mechanics right now and the first month is free — no card required, just the promo code SRITORQUED when you sign up. After that it's $99/month flat, which is less than the profit on one oil change."**

> **"Takes about 10 minutes to set up online. Want me to send you the link so you can have a look after the call?"**

---

## EMAIL FOLLOW-UP TRIGGER

If yes:
> **"What's the best email for you? I'll send it right now."**

If not sure:
> **"No pressure — I'll send it over and you can look when it suits. If you've got any questions I'm on [your number]. Cheers, [Name]."**

---

## OBJECTIONS

| Objection | Response |
|---|---|
| "We're full already" | "Good to hear — the platform's still useful for filling the occasional gap. And you can pause anytime." |
| "We don't need more work" | "Understood. The other thing mechanics use it for is the quoting tool — builds and emails itemised PDFs in under a minute. Worth a look if you quote manually now." |
| "Sounds too good to be true" | "Fair enough — that's why the first month's free. No card, no commitment. Worst case you get a few extra jobs for nothing." |
| "What if the customer doesn't show?" | "They pay a deposit upfront — you keep a portion even on a no-show based on your own cancellation policy." |
| "I'm not techy" | "The portal's one page. If you can use TradeMe you can use this." |
