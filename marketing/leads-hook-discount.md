# Torqued — Leads Hook & Discount Strategy

---

## THE HOOK

Use this as the core value prop across all outreach:

> **"Get your first 30 days free — no card. Booked jobs from Dunedin customers, 4% on completion. Code: SRITORQUED."**

Short. Specific. Low-risk ask.

---

## DISCOUNT TIERS

| Tier | Offer | Code | Who gets it |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Trial** | 30-day free trial, no card | `SRITORQUED` | All new mechanics |
| **Referral** | 2 months free | `REFER2` | Referred by an active mechanic |
| **Beta Partner** | 3 months free + priority listing | Direct outreach only | First 5 workshops per suburb |

---

## HIGH-VALUE LEAD TARGETS (Dunedin)

### Tier 1 — Highest conversion likelihood
- Independent mechanics with 3–10 Google reviews (established but not oversized)
- Mobile mechanics (no fixed bay cost = high margin, love flexible bookings)
- Specialists: Euro, EV, 4WD — customers actively search for them
- Mechanics running social media but no online booking

### Tier 2 — Good leads
- MTA-certified workshops without a website
- Workshops listed on AA Smartfuel but no booking system
- Caravan & marine mechanics (niche, underserved)

### Tier 3 — Harder to convert (but still worth trying)
- Franchise workshops (need head-office approval)
- Very high-volume shops (may not need more work)

---

## WHERE TO FIND LEADS

1. **Google Maps** — search "mechanic Dunedin", "WOF Dunedin", "car service Mosgiel" etc. Click every result with fewer than 50 reviews.
2. **Facebook Groups** — "Dunedin Buy, Sell, Trade", "Dunedin Community Notice Board" — mechanics often post there
3. **Trademe Services** — filter by Otago, look for auto mechanics
4. **AA Approved Repairers list** — public directory, free to scrape names
5. **NZTA Vehicle Inspection site** — lists all licensed WOF inspectors by region

## URGENCY / SCARCITY LEVERS

Use these sparingly — only when true:

- "We're only onboarding [X] mechanics per suburb before we close the area"
- "The free trial ends [date] — after that it's back to a 7-day trial"
- "We're running a beta programme for the first 10 mechanics — you'd get a featured placement on the map"
