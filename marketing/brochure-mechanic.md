# TORQUED — Mechanic Partner Brochure
### NZ's smarter way to fill your workshop bays

---

## WHAT IS TORQUED?

Torqued is a Dunedin-built platform that connects car owners with trusted local mechanics. Customers book online, pay upfront, and you get the job — no phone tag, no quoting wars, no no-shows without a deposit.

---

## HOW IT WORKS

**1. Customer books your workshop**
They find you on Torqued, choose their service, select a date, and pay a deposit. You get a real-time notification with all the details.

**2. You manage it in one place**
The Mechanic Portal shows every incoming job, your calendar, parts orders, and revenue — all in one tab.

**3. Job done, you get paid**
Once complete, Torqued pays out your earnings weekly. We take 4% on completion — that's it.

---

## WHAT YOU GET

**Booked jobs, not leads**
Customers arrive having already paid a deposit. No chasing. No ghosting.

**Itemised Quote Builder**
For diagnostic jobs, build a professional quote inside Torqued. It emails a branded PDF to the customer — they tap a link to accept and pay. Takes 60 seconds.

**Vehicle History per Job**
Every car comes with its service history — imported records + all previous Torqued jobs. Know what you're working with before they arrive.

**AI Mechanic Assistant**
Ask for oil capacities, torque specs, cambelt intervals, OEM part numbers. In seconds.

**AI Parts Lookup**
Type a part name, get the OEM number and estimated NZD price from local suppliers.

**Calendar & Capacity Control**
Set your technician count, parts lead time, and cancellation policy. Torqued shows customers realistic turnaround times automatically.

**Service Packages**
Build up to 3 fixed-price packages (e.g. Basic Service, Full Service, Transmission Service). Customers can book these directly — no quoting needed.

**Branded Invoices & Reports**
Download a Torqued-branded tax invoice for any paid job. Email it in one click.
